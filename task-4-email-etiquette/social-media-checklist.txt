Social Media Checklist

Do's
Think before you post Consider how you may be affected by your post.
Keep your profiles professional, particularly LinkedIn.
Check any information before posting it to prevent the dissemination of false information.
Respect the opinions of others, and engage in healthy discussions.
Use privacy settings to manage who can see your content
Don'ts
Do not expose personal and sensitive information in the public.
Don't do online harassment, online trolling, or harassment.
Don't send inappropriate and offensive content.
Don't believe or further whatever without fact checking.
Don't spend too much time on scrolling - be wise of your screen time.
