# An online safety precaution checklist.
* For all accounts use strong and unique passwords and do not reuse passwords.
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your important accounts such as your email, banking, and social media.
* Never ever give anyone your OTP, UPI PIN or details your banking details like UPI numbers with anybody else even if they claim to be from a bank.
* Do not scan QR Codes they take from unknown sources or complete unexpected requests for payment (UPI safety).
* Always ensure the details of a receiver and then sending money through UPI apps (UPI safety).
* Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email or on social media.
* Get applications only through reliable applications such as Google Play Store or App Store.
* Keep your devices patched and updated with the latest security updates as well as antivirus protection.
* Be careful while using public Wi-Fi do not log in into sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
* Look through your account statements and transactions on a regular basis to identify any activity that is suspicious.
